Great Wall of China, China

Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications made of stone, brick, tamped earth, wood, and other materials. It was built along the northern borders of China to protect against various nomadic groups.

Stretching over 13,000 miles, the Great Wall of China is one of the most impressive architectural feats in history. It was primarily built during the Ming Dynasty and remains a popular tourist attraction.
