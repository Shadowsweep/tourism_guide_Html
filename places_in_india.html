Top Tourist Places in India

Taj Mahal, Agra

Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World, located in Agra, India. It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Jaipur - The Pink City

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known for its rich history, royal architecture, and vibrant culture. The Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and Amber Fort are must-visit places.

Goa - Beaches and Sunsets

Goa is famous for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. It's a perfect destination for beach lovers and party enthusiasts.
